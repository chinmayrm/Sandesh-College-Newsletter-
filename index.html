About the College Newsletter

The Sandesh newsletter captures the academic, cultural, and research milestones of Basaveshwar Engineering College, Bagalkot. It is published monthly by the editorial team in collaboration with faculty and student representatives. Each edition highlights institutional achievements, department activities, student success stories, and upcoming initiatives. Editorial team details and contributions will be updated here for every academic year.
